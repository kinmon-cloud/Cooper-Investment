Human Resources Coordinator

Location: Fort Worth, TX (Hybrid – 3 days onsite, 2 days remote)
Employment Type: Full-time, Exempt

About Us

At Cooper Property Investment, we believe people are the heart of our success. We are a growing property investment firm dedicated to helping our employees grow. We’re looking for a motivated, experienced HR Coordinator to join our team and help us shape a workplace where employees thrive.

Role Overview

As an HR Coordinator, you will support daily HR operations, assist with recruitment and onboarding, and serve as a first point of contact for employee inquiries. This role is perfect for someone who is detail-oriented, people-focused, and eager to grow their HR career.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with posting jobs, scheduling interviews, and coordinating candidate communications.
  • Support new hire onboarding, including preparing materials and conducting orientations.
  • Maintain accurate employee records in HR systems.
  • Coordinate training sessions and employee engagement activities.
  • Respond to general HR-related questions with professionalism and confidentiality.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.
  • 1–2 years of HR or administrative experience preferred.
  • Strong organizational and communication skills.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite; HRIS experience a plus.
  • Ability to handle sensitive information with discretion.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary ($55,000–$75,000, depending on experience).
  • Hybrid work schedule with flexible hours.
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance starting after 30 days.
  • 401(k) with company match.
  • Paid time off, volunteer days, and professional development opportunities.
